## RULES BEFORE YOU POST

**Always include:**
- ✅ A personal POV or observation — not a press release
- ✅ One clear CTA with a working link
- ✅ Currency formatted correctly: $59/month, $4,900/year, $48,000
- ✅ Humour or a pattern interrupt in the first line
- ✅ Tag the product: Reading Buddy by Outcome Ready
- ✅ End with the link + one sentence on what clicking does

**Never:**
- ❌ Start with "I'm excited to announce"
- ❌ Wall of text — max 5 lines before a line break
- ❌ Buzzwords: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "disrupting"
- ❌ Paste AI copy without reading it aloud first
- ❌ Post without checking the link actually opens
